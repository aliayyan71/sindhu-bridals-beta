Return Policy

Returns

To raise a dispute regarding a defective item, please email clear pictures of the product to example@gmail.com or contact us at +92 0000000000 within 7 days of receiving your order.

All disputes will be reviewed by Shazia Sindhu, who will ensure a fair and balanced resolution for both parties.

Once we receive the item, our team will assess the reported damage. If the dispute is resolved in your favor, a replacement of equal value (as per the invoice) will be issued and shipped to the address provided.

Please note, you will be responsible for all return shipping charges. We strongly recommend that you use a trackable method to mail your return.

Please Note:

  • In case of an exchange, the customer is responsible for all shipping and handling costs.
  • Discounted items are non-returnable and non-exchangeable.
  • Unstitched items that have been stitched will not be eligible for return or exchange.
  • Wedding wear and couture dresses are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged under any circumstances.
